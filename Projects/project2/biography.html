BIOGRAPHY

DJ TERRIBLE

Hover over for a secret message.

DJ Terrible is based off the cold lands of Canada where they is constantly inspired by the sounds of construction and destruction. Their hometown in Montreal, Quebec is where they started their career as a recorder player in elementary school. They were terrible at it. Their professors discouraged them of any career in music because all the other students and faculty complained of the horrible bird noises when in fact it was DJ Terrible attempting to play their first instrument.

Not long after, they auditioned in their high school band and they failed miserably. The judges were not impressed of his rendition of Celine Dion's "My Heart Will Go On" on the recorder. Disappointed, they left the audition with a hope to find a community accepting of their artistic vision.

At age 21, DJ Terrible stumbled across Youtube for the first time and they came across the official video of "Axel F" from Crazy Frog. The upbeat, auto-tuned and crazy looking frog was his main inspiration to start music production. Today, they continue to practice making new tunes (which you can on this website!) and practice the recorder on a daily basis in hopes to find their first venue to perform live. Please consider hiring DJ Terrible at your next bar mitzvah, wedding anniversary party or workplace corporate gathering. DJ Terrible's goal is to bring the Terrible to the table!
